Whats New

  • New School Canteen

    The school canteen is a great place to promote an enjoyment of healthy eating. For students who use the canteen regularly, the food purchased makes a significant contribution to their total food intake and nutrition; therefore it makes sense to ensure the best food possible is available to enhance their ability to learn and take in the information presented to them in class

  • New School Swimming Pool

    School pools are the place for children to improve their physical fitness. They also serve as educational facilities for young children to test their assertiveness, sense of accomplishment, and other possibilities. Yamaha uses its wealth of expertise accumulated over 30 years of pool building to offer a wide range of pool facilities made to suit the needs of educational institutions that are safe and make teaching easy.
